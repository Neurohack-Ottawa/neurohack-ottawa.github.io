Neurohack Ottawa

Computational neuroscience is an exciting, multidisciplinary field that combines neuroscience, physics, and computer science to unlock the secrets of the brain. In this 24-hour hackathon students are given a chance to attend workshops, form teams, and put together a project involving neuroscience and programming!

Learn

  • Students in the life sciences will be able to learn about coding (something that is missing from a lot of undergrad programs in Ottawa)
  • Students in physical sciences will learn about neuroscience and how to apply their quantitative skills

Connect

  • Students can learn about research happening around Ottawa.
  • Researchers can open source their data and use the hackathon for recruitment.
  • Sponsors can increase their presence in the Ottawa neuroscience community and/or use the hackathon for recruitment.

Methods

Interdisciplinary

Bring together a diverse group of participants by connecting different departments and schools in the Ottawa region.

Collaboration

Participants will be organized into teams and will have access to various neuroscience datasets, as well as computational tools and resources.

Workshops

Workshops and lectures will provide background on core topics, such as an introduction to Python, simulation of neuronal dynamics, and machine learning.

Awards

Teams will present their projects to a panel of judges, with the chance to receive award prizes.
