Neurohack Ottawa

Computational neuroscience is an exciting, multidisciplinary field that combines neuroscience, physics, and computer science to unlock the secrets of the brain.

In this 24-hour hackathon students are given a chance to attend workshops, form teams, and put together a project involving neuroscience and programming!

Learn

  • Develop coding skills of students in the life sciences
  • Discover new applications of skills for students in physical sciences

Connect

  • Students with students
  • Students with exciting research happening around Ottawa
  • Research groups with passionate students
  • Sponsors with talented graduating students

Methods

Growth

Gather participants with diverse backgrounds and interests.

Collaboration

Students create small teams and decide which projects to work on.

Workshops

Introduce valuable topics such as Python, simulation of neuronal dynamics, and machine learning.

Awards

Teams will present their projects to a panel of judges.

Outcomes & Conclusion

The goal of Neurohack Ottawa is to provide a platform for interdisciplinary collaboration, encourage the development of novel computational tools and methods, and foster the training of the next generation of neuroscientists.
